System Overview

Our electroflotation system uses controlled current and voltage to generate microbubbles, lifting suspended particles and algae to the surface. Closed‑loop control maintains optimal operating conditions based on turbidity and power consumption.

Reactor Design

Vertical acrylic column with adjustable electrode spacing, optimized for bubble residence time and flow distribution.

Power & Control

Programmable supply with current limiting, integrated sensors, and data logging for voltage, current, and turbidity.

Measurement

Turbidity, bubble size distribution, and energy per unit mass of water treated.

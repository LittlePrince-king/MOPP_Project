MOPP

Mosquito & Organism Purification Project

Removing the root cause of mosquito breeding.

Mosquitoes breed on the still algal film of stagnant water. The MOPP system aggregates and sinks that surface algae cutting off the breeding layer while leaving the pond's biosphere intact.
